ALPHAWALK PRIVACY POLICY

This privacy policy (“Policy”) applies to personal information we hold on our users, people who visit our websites (including without limitation, https://alphawalk.ai), and anyone else whose personal data we hold.

Last updated · 23 July 2026 · support@alphawalk.ai

In this Policy, “we,” “us,” and “our” means Quantummind Solutions Hong Kong Limited. This Policy deals with where we are acting as a ‘data controller’ under the data protection legislation: which essentially means where we are responsible for deciding what personal data we collect and how we use it. We may at times operate as a ‘data processor’, meaning that we are processing personal data on behalf of another entity. When we refer to “personal data”, we mean data relating to you personally or from which you can be identified. Some of the information mentioned in this Policy is not “personal data” as is meant under data protection legislation, but we may refer to it to help you understand what we do.

We are committed to protecting and respecting the personal information of everyone we deal with – that is important to us and we know it is important to you. In this Policy you will find information about what types of personal data we hold about you, how and why we collect it, how we use it, and how we keep it secure. You will also find the information about your rights and how you may exercise them.

If you are younger than 18 years of age (or age of majority otherwise determined in your country of residency if the age of majority in your country of residency is older than 18 years old), you are not allowed to access our website, mobile app, products and services.

Please read this Policy carefully and if you need to contact us about anything that is in here, or for anything else related to your personal data, you can reach us by email at support@alphawalk.ai.

WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT ABOUT YOU?

The type of personal data we collect about you depends on your relationship with us. In general, we collect the following information:

A. Personal data that you provide directly to us:

  1. your contact information such as your name and email address;
  2. your account / display name and password;
  3. your login credentials when you register with us;
  4. data that you provided to us to verify your account;
  5. other data you choose to provide us, such as data to identify a lost or lapsed account or the answer to a special question we may ask you in order to permit you to unlock or recover a lost or lapsed account;
  6. information about your purchases and payments (in addition, exclusively our payment service providers will collect your payment details in connection with any purchases you make in our products or services);
  7. personal data you provide in connection with surveys, promotions, sweepstakes, competitions, activities, campaigns and other events; and
  8. other personal data that may be collected based on your explicit consent or our legitimate business interest for a specific purpose.

B. Personal data that we collect automatically when you access our products or services:

  1. your profile in our services, including your profile picture and any information needed for the relevant service to function, such as an inventory of service credits;
  2. your product / service user ID(s) – product / service-specific ID(s) generated by our server for you;
  3. Analytics ID – a randomly generated anonymous identifier used to distinguish activity from one service / product account versus another in our analytics data;
  4. your account ID and its related account and profile information (including without limitation the profile picture) from third party platforms (e.g. Google, Apple), including when such account details is used in connection with our products or services, or used in connection with surveys, promotions, sweepstakes, competitions, activities, campaigns and other events arranged, administered, facilitated, managed or organized by us (whether solely or jointly with others);
  5. your payment and credit card/bank account details, when such payment and credit card/bank account details is used in connection with our products or services, or used in connection with surveys, promotions, sweepstakes, competitions, activities, campaigns and other events arranged, administered, facilitated, managed or organized by us (whether solely or jointly with others). We do not currently automatically collect this data under our ALPHAWALK service and only limited transaction data is received by us through Stripe, but we may choose to automatically collect this in the future;
  6. your internet protocol (IP) address;
  7. your country, time zone, and locale/region settings (we do not currently automatically collect this data, but may access and collect it as and when required by us. We may also choose to automatically collect it in the future);
  8. data to fight fraud (such as refund abuse in products or services, or click fraud in advertising);
  9. usage data including usage logs, and information about how you use our website, products, and services;
  10. service data including in the context of our ALPHAWALK service, your watchlist, preferences and settings; the prompts you submit to agents and the reports/outputs generated for you; your agent hire history and credit balance and usage;
  11. data about your device, such as device name and operating system, browser type, and language; and
  12. marketing and communications data including your preferences in receiving marketing from us and our third parties and your communication preferences and it is at your full own discretion and control to adjust the settings on your device to turn off the function.

C. Personal data that we collect from third parties:

  1. third party account ID and related account and profile information (including without limitation the profile picture), as well as payment and credit card/bank account details, when such account and/or payment and credit card/bank account details is used in connection with our products or services, or used in connection with surveys, promotions, sweepstakes, competitions, activities, campaigns and other events arranged, administered, facilitated, managed or organized by us (whether solely or jointly with others);
  2. if you connect the ALPHAWALK service through Telegram, Lark and/or third party email, we receive and store, as applicable: for Telegram, the chat ID of the chat you connect (obtained when Telegram delivers back the one-time binding code during QR-based setup; for group chats we also briefly process the chat type and group title); for Lark, the incoming-webhook URL (and optional signing secret) you supply; and for email, the recipient address you supply, verified via a confirmation email;
  3. payment confirmation details, including the purchases you make and the amount you spend. Specifically in relation to the ALPHAWALK service, payments under the ALPHAWALK service are processed by Stripe. We receive limited transaction data (amount, currency, last four card digits, transaction status) for payments which you make for the ALPHAWALK service - we never receive or store your full card number, and Stripe's processing is governed by its own privacy policy; and
  4. if you are a Creator receiving earnings under the ALPHAWALK service, our payout partner(s) will collect identity-verification (KYC) and bank/tax information directly from you; we receive verification status and the information needed from our payout partner(s) to account for your earnings.
